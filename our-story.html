Mandalay Wellbeing CIC & Trevor Saunders


Mandalay Wellbeing CIC was founded by its Director, Trevor Saunders, In 2017. Following many years of voluntary work including being a Brisley Parish Council Member, where he set up and ran Brisley Youth Club, Trevor progressed to be a Member of the National Association Of Town and Parish Councils and the National Association Of Playing Fields. More recently Trevor has become Vice Chairman of Great Yarmouth Older Peoples Network and works with Great Yarmouth Dementia Action Alliance. As a voluntary member of East Norfolk Medical Practice's Patient Participation Group Trevor organised Community Engagement Events which are designed to bring local support groups and organisations together with GPs, Clinicians and Patients enabling each other to gain a better understanding of the services available in the community enabling excellent outcomes and building strong relationships.

Mandalay Wellbeing CIC was created from the experience gained from this work and now offers a range of projects developed from both personal life experience and learnings from his voluntary work.

Trevor has also worked as an Area Manager for Post Office Ltd, Brand and Sales Manager for Saab, Honda and Audi Automobiles and many years ago was Reserve Goalkeeper for Norwich City Football Club and a Bluecoat at Caister Holiday Camp. He is now a Lay Member on the Governing Body for Great Yarmouth and Waveney Clinical Commissioning Group specialising in Public and Patient Involvement. “Mandalay Wellbeing CIC has developed with the support of many volunteers and the help and advice of professionals both on our board and from external sources”. “It has been a massive step and a huge personal challenge to get to where we are today”; “We are always looking for help and ideas to improve and as an organisation we are delighted to get to where we are today but realise we have a long way to go to reach our goals”.
